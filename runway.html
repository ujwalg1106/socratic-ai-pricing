Runway Health
Current Runway iMonths of cash remaining at your current net burn rate. Formula: Cash ÷ (Gross Burn − MRR). 🔴 <6 mo 🟡 6–12 mo 🟢 ≥18 mo — months remaining
Net Burn Rate iMonthly cash consumption after subtracting all revenue. Formula: Gross Burn − Total MRR. Negative means you're cash-flow positive — revenue exceeds expenses. — per month
ARR at Month 12 iAnnualized Run Rate — projected MRR at month 12 multiplied by 12. Key milestones investors track: $500k → $1M → $3M → $10M ARR. — annualized
Break-even Month iThe month when MRR first equals or exceeds Gross Burn. After this point, operations no longer consume cash — you're self-sustaining. — MRR = Gross Burn
Raise Needed iAdditional capital required to fund your target runway, beyond current cash and projected revenue. Formula: (Burn × Target Months) − Cash − Projected Revenue − Planned Raise. — for target runway
Unit Economics
B2B LTV : CAC iLifetime Value ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost for enterprise clients. LTV = Deal Revenue × Avg Lifetime × Gross Margin. Benchmark ≥ 3:1. Below 1:1 means you lose money on every client. — benchmark ≥ 3 : 1
B2C LTV : CAC iLifetime Value ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost for individual subscribers. LTV = Monthly Price × Avg Lifetime × Gross Margin. Benchmark ≥ 3:1. — benchmark ≥ 3 : 1
NRR — B2B iNet Revenue Retention — % of B2B revenue retained after 12 months, including upsells and minus churn. Above 100% means existing clients alone grow your revenue. The #1 metric Series B+ investors look at. — benchmark ≥ 100%
B2B CAC Payback iMonths to recover your enterprise client acquisition cost from gross margin. Formula: CAC ÷ (Monthly Revenue × Gross Margin %). Benchmark <12 months. — benchmark < 12 mo
Magic Number iMeasures S&M efficiency. Formula: Net New ARR (year 1) ÷ Annual S&M Spend. ≥0.75 = efficient, ≥1.0 = excellent. Below 0.25 means your sales engine is not working. — benchmark ≥ 0.75
Rule of 40 iYoY revenue growth % + gross margin %. A healthy SaaS scores ≥40. Fast-growing early-stage companies often score 80–150+. Below 40 signals you need to either grow faster or cut costs. — growth + margin
Quick Ratio iGrowth efficiency score. Formula: (New MRR + Expansion MRR) ÷ Churned MRR at month 12. >4 = excellent, 1–4 = growing, <1 = shrinking. Measures how much new revenue you generate per dollar lost to churn. — new + expansion ÷ churn
ARPU at M12 iAverage Revenue Per User at month 12. Formula: Total MRR ÷ Total Active Customers. Higher ARPU = more revenue per customer, easier path to profitability. — avg revenue / customer
Total Customers M12 iCombined count of active enterprise clients and individual subscribers at month 12, after accounting for growth and churn in both streams. — B2B clients + B2C subs
B2C CAC Payback iMonths to recover your subscriber acquisition cost from gross margin. Formula: CAC ÷ (Monthly Price × Gross Margin %). Benchmark <6 months for consumer SaaS. — benchmark < 6 mo
